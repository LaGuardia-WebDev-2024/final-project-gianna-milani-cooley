Food&Drinks I love

Mac and Cheese

I absolutely love mac and cheese because it's warm, cheesy, and super comforting. The creamy texture and gooey melted cheese make it one of my all-time favorite dishes.

Sushi

Sushi is another favorite of mine—it's fresh, flavorful, and comes in so many varieties! I especially enjoy salmon rolls and avocado maki for their unique taste and textures.
